Main Fig. 2 | Retention and loss of 30 m contrail signals in kilometre-scale sensors. The hero scene is KX10_TIS_20230531_W72.75_N5.11_202300357274_L4B. The mapped panels show an automatically selected 85.0 km by 85.0 km hero ROI containing 8 retained and 10 missed objects.
a, SDGSAT-1 30 m BTD reference with the native 30 m contrail-mask outline. b, Sensor-specific simulations produced by averaging the SDGSAT-1 30 m BTD to the MODIS 1 km and GOES ABI H10 2 km grids. c, Real MODIS and GOES ABI BTD observations over the same spatial extent; the GOES panel uses H10 parallax-corrected object support. d, Retained/missed classification of 30 m objects in the real products: green, detected by both sensors; blue, MODIS only; orange, GOES H10 only; red, missed by both. e, Morphology-dependent detection retention with scene-level bootstrap 95% confidence intervals. f, Relationship between pixel fill proxy and signal retention; points show object-level measurements and lines show binned median trends.
